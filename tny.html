Single-panel cartoons published in The New Yorker
2020-2024

I have submitted cartoons to every weekly New Yorker cartoon meeting since September 2019 (I submitted monthly before then for about a year, and there are some weeks with no cartoon meeting) and will never stop. I highly encourage everyone to start submitting. If someone decides to draw, even if they never get published anywhere, no one can really stop them.
